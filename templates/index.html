Back in the 90's, Moose Leather was a leather store in sunny San Diego, CA that specialized in the creation custom-made leather goods. We also carried off-the-rack merchandise as well as consignment of people's leather items on which we received a commission when the items sold.

This website is an attempt to create an online version of the shop. There is retail section called New Goods and, just like the store, there are used items for sale in the section called Consigned Goods.

Each item for sale has a History and Background page connected to it. Just click the title to see the history of that type of leather item. Also, each page has a discussion section at the bottom if you want to ask a question or offer an opinion...
